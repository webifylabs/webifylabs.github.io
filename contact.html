Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your development process?

We follow an agile methodology with regular check-ins, iterative development, and continuous feedback. This ensures transparency and allows for adjustments throughout the project.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! We offer various maintenance and support packages to ensure your project continues to run smoothly after launch. We're here for the long haul.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a wide range of modern technologies including React, Vue, Node.js, Python, and more. We choose the best tools for each project's specific needs.
